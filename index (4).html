About the academy

A COMMUNITY FOUNDED ON INTEGRITY, HARD WORK AND MUTUAL RESPECT.

Our school is dedicated to providing quality education that empowers learners academically, socially, and emotionally. We pride ourselves on a safe and supportive environment, qualified and passionate teachers, and a strong focus on both classroom learning and extracurricular development. The school promotes diversity, inclusion, and equal opportunities for all learners to discover their unique strengths..

We promote diversity, inclusion and equal opportunities for all learners to discover their unique strengths — through mentorship, guidance and counseling that ensure the holistic development of every student.

Our Mission

To offer quality education through a supportive environment, competent staff and well-structured programs that promote academic excellence, character development and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To provide holistic education that nurtures responsible, knowledgeable and innovative learners who are prepared to thrive in a dynamic world.

Our Declaration

We — students, staff and stakeholders — commit ourselves to uphold the values, rules and standards that foster respect, discipline and academic excellence.

Facilities

A place where every learner can thrive.

From the classroom to the playing field, every space on campus is designed to support curiosity, character and growth.

A serene, green school

Lush lawns, shaded trees and flower gardens make our compound a calm, inspiring place to learn.

Modern classrooms

Bright, well-equipped rooms designed for focused learning and collaborative work.

A well-stocked library

A quiet space for reading, research and the love of discovery to take root.

Modern science lab

Reagents, Bunsen burners and microscopes — practical science with proper safety standards.

Technology & ICT lab

Modern computers and high-speed internet for typing, research, coding and digital creativity.

Kitchen & dining hall

Spacious, hygienic dining with nutritious, freshly prepared meals every school day.

Sports & playgrounds

Open fields for football, volleyball and athletics build teamwork, discipline and fitness.

Achievement & Innovation

Highlighting our students' accomplishments and fostering a culture of innovation.

Farming & Agriculture
